About Me

// who I am

I'm a Graphics & Games Programmer with a deep passion for real-time rendering, GPU programming, and interactive experiences. My career spans some of the most respected names in the industry — from writing display driver test suites at NVIDIA, to designing lighting systems at Ubisoft, to shipping UI for Forza Horizon 5 at Sumo Digital.

My expertise lives at the boundary between hardware and software — where pixels meet pipelines. I understand the DirectX 11 and OpenGL rendering pipelines deeply, and I've written tests, tools, and features that run at the driver, engine, and application layers.

I believe in writing clean, performant code that respects the hardware underneath. When I'm not coding, I'm studying the latest in real-time GI, shader techniques, and game architecture.
